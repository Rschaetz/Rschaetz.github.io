The Compass of the Heart
This compass I want to give to you.
A compass? you may think…For what to do?
Listen, I will explain to you.

The compass of your heart shows the way to inner home.
No matter in what situation you are, it will never leave you alone.
It shall guide you the way when you feel there´s no light.
It will take you away from fear, make you feel bright.
Whenever you feel like loosing your way,
listen to the compass of your heart, it can turn the dark night into day.
It always knows the right direction,
follow, trust and you will see,
listen to your heart makes you feel free.
I give this "compass of the heart " to you.
It will always tell you: I love you
